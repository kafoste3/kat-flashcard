About
Foster's Flashers allows anyone to create quick study guides using digital flashcards!
We are dedicated to promoting brain power by flashing knowledge you want to know! You can create, edit, and manage flashcards for any subject!
How It Works
Your brain is a muscle so EXERCISE IT!
By using a reaction of flashing a question, we are forcing your brain to be reflexive! This means it will call information from the jumble of a noodle in your head faster, clearer, and flashier!
